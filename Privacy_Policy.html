Privacy Policy

1. Data We Collect

We collect the following data to provide the core functionality of the ScamGuard Vouch Bot:

  • Discord User IDs
  • Vouch Records (who vouched for whom, timestamp, message)
  • Scammer Reports (ID, reason, timestamp)
  • Guild and Channel IDs for configuration

We DO NOT collect:

  • IP addresses
  • Emails or passwords
  • Direct messages (DMs)
  • Location data

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to:

  • Manage the vouch system
  • Track and display scammer reports
  • Generate user lookup profiles

3. Data Sharing

We do not share personal data with third parties. Only authorized developers have access to the data for maintenance and support purposes.

4. Data Retention

Vouch and report data is stored indefinitely unless removed by the bot owner or by user request.

5. Security Measures

We implement encryption, secure hosting, and access restrictions. However, no system is 100% secure.

6. Your Rights
